## ğŸ¨ Design Principles

### Core Values
1. **Professional & Trustworthy** - Legal services require high trust
2. **Clean & Minimal** - Focus on content, reduce cognitive load
3. **iOS-First** - Native iOS feel with Cupertino widgets
4. **Accessible** - WCAG 2.1 AA compliance
5. **Fast & Responsive** - Instant feedback on user actions

### Design Language
- **Style**: Modern, clean, professional
- **Tone**: Authoritative but friendly
- **Target Users**: Business owners, legal teams, entrepreneurs (25-55 years old)
